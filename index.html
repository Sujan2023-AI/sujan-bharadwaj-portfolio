About Me

Hello! I'm an AI & Data Science Engineer with ~3 years of experience specializing in transforming complex data into actionable business insights. My passion lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, sustainability analytics, and ethical AI development.

Currently pursuing my Master's in AI at UNSW Sydney, I've built my expertise around ESG knowledge graphs, scalable data pipelines, and deploying ML models that drive real-world impact. My work spans from designing Large Concept Models for scientific literature analysis to developing ontology-enhanced ESG analytics platforms that help organizations make data-driven sustainability decisions.

What sets me apart is my focus on AI Ethics and Responsible Data Science - ensuring that the models I build not only perform well but also contribute positively to society. I'm particularly interested in how AI can solve sustainability challenges and create transparent, interpretable systems that stakeholders can trust.

My technical toolkit includes Python, PyTorch, AWS, SPARQL, and modern data visualization tools. But beyond the technology, I'm driven by the mission to bridge the gap between academic innovation and practical business solutions.
